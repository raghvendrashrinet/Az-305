## 1: Azure Architect Decision Framework
---
```
Business Requirement
        │
        ▼
Who needs access?
        │
        ▼
How should they authenticate?
        │
        ▼
What permissions do they need?
        │
        ▼
How will resources be governed?
        │
        ▼
How will secrets be stored?
        │
        ▼
How will everything be monitored?
```
